We design products for us...

Zookyo started in 2010 as an online store of premium Japanese products. A few years and many experiences after that we decided to start building products that we wanted ourselves. We are nomads, filmmakers and love tech, so it only made sense that we tried to build the best gear for mobile filmmakers and photographers out there.

...but we build them for you!

We realized that there were many people like us with the same needs and wishes. So we decidede to make the best possible products for people on the move that want to take their art to the next level without having to spend a fortune and in the smaller sizes possible. We also believe that those amazing super computers and media creation devices we have in our pockets will only get better and better.
